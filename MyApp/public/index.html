About

I love Spotify's custom "On Repeat" and "Repeat Rewind" playlists, but I wanted them to be longer and I wanted playlists representing my listening habits from a longer period of time. So I developed a this tool that automatically creates playlists based off of a user's top songs from different time periods. It updates the playlists automatically and keeps them at 100 songs each.

Privacy Policy

I securely store your spotify username and two tokens that spotify gives me when you login. These tokens are used to prove to spotify that you gave me permission to access your spotify data. I can't make playlists for you without them. If you would like me to delete them (and no longer update the playlists I've generated for you), you can do that easily by clicking the clear data button. You will need to login to do so.
